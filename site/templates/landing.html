Make a Face!


Play
How to play: It's simple. You'll see a face appear on the right side of your screen.
All you have to do is mimic that face to score a point! For each face, try to mimic a given face as quickly as possible.
You'll also see a scale of emotional and positional coefficients. These are calculated relative to the emotions and positions of the face shown in the lower right hand corner. Use these as a guide to help you form your facial expressions.
When the average of the emotional and positional coefficients surpass a threshold of 60%, you get a point!
You can also scroll through different faces to choose one you'd like to imitate. Have fun!
